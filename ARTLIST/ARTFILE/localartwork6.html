Hymn of the Earth

Persemonn

2025

As the Earth gradually cries for help, we humans should become a channel of communication that heeds its call. The hymn of the Earth will reverberate through the cracks, each pulse imploring for help to come at last

About Artist

Vincent Van Gogh

Persemonn is an artist based in Tarlac City who channels raw emotion and the astounding world they have lived into abstract compositions. Their art captures the turbulence of human emotions creating a dialogue between their audience. They aim to share their work towards the world by actively contributing to one's awareness towards artistic expression.
