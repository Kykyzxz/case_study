The Starry Night

Vincent Van Gogh

1889

The Starry Night depicts a dreamy interpretation of the artist’s asylum room’s sweeping view of Saint-Rémy-de-Provence at night. It's one of Van Gogh’s most recognized works, capturing emotion through swirling skies and bold colors. About Artist

Vincent Van Gogh

Bonjour, I’m Vincent van Gogh. You might know me for my swirling skies and sun-drenched fields—I painted with passion, often straight from the soul. Though my life was filled with struggle, I found beauty in the everyday: a café at night, a vase of sunflowers, the quiet of a starry evening. I didn’t sell many paintings while alive, but today, people say my art speaks louder than words. I painted not just what I saw, but what I felt. Welcome to my world—where color is emotion and brushstrokes are poetry.
