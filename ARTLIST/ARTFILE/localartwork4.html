About Artwork

Two View

Cesiah Gerona

2024

The piece is about the sunset which often symbolizes the end of a cycle and the start of a new one. It tells people to not focus on the failure of yesterday but to start today with positive thoughts and expectations.

About Artist

Cesiah Gerona

Cesiah is an artist based in Tarlac City who creates art based on the things she loves. She works in Bayong Gracia which is a small business bayong bags where she paints on Bayong bags and pouches.
