About Artwork

Adrian Galsim

2025

The piece resembling a being that embodies the struggle of the artist, the devil or the angel— an entity torn between divine and desire. The skull depicts that he's already dead inside but the blood is the resemblance of the struggle of being something. The eyes searches for truth, desperate to understand himself. Stripped bare, he stands with no defense — honest and vulnerable but has a will of a champion. As he reach through the sky — longing to ascend, know everything, and be something but deep down he cannot be the all knowing. The clothes clings to him as is all that's left on his purity, a reminder that his clothes is the only thing that hides him to his cruel sanity.
