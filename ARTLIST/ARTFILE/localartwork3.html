About Artist

Allen Ferdinald Torres

Allen Ferdinald Torres is a 20-year-old artist from Tarlac City. Currently enrolled in a college program that doesn't align with his interests, he finds his true calling in the arts, where he channels his creativity and authentic self-expression. Despite the disconnect between his academic path and personal aspirations, he continues to pursue artistic work that reflects his genuine passion, proving that sometimes the most meaningful journeys happen outside the classroom. His art serves as both an escape and a declaration of who he truly is and hopes to become.
